About Roland Institute of Technology

Roland Institute of Technology, Berhampur is one of the leading engineering colleges in Odisha. It is affiliated to BPUT, Rourkela and approved by AICTE.

The institute offers quality education in engineering, management, and computer applications with modern infrastructure and experienced faculty.

Why Choose Our College?

  • ✔ Highly qualified and experienced faculty members
  • ✔ Modern infrastructure with smart classrooms
  • ✔ Well-equipped laboratories for practical learning
  • ✔ Strong placement support with top companies
  • ✔ Digital library with thousands of resources
  • ✔ Hostel and transport facilities available
  • ✔ Industry-oriented curriculum and training
  • ✔ Regular workshops, seminars, and hackathons
